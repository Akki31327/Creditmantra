Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Credit Bureau?

A credit bureau tracks how you handle money, like paying loans or bills on time. Lenders check this to see if they should give you a loan. In India, the main credit bureaus are CIBIL, Experian, Equifax, and CRIF.

Why Does Your CIBIL Score Matter?

Your CIBIL score shows how well you manage credit. A high score makes it easier to get loans at lower interest rates, while a low score can make borrowing more expensive and difficult.

What is a Credit Score?

A credit score is a number that reflects your credit management habits. It ranges from 300 to 900, with higher scores indicating better creditworthiness. Lenders use your credit score to decide whether to approve your loan applications.

How Will CreditMantra's CIBIL Improvement Package Help Me?

CreditMantra analyzes your credit report and loan details, providing insights on loan eligibility and personalized improvement suggestions. You can file disputes for inaccuracies, ensuring your credit report is accurate. The automated dispute resolution process keeps you informed at every step.
